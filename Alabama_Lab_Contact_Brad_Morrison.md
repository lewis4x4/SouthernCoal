## What Brad Does (His Role)

Brad is **not** the lab — he is the **sampler and coordinator** for Alabama mine water sampling. His workflow:

1. Collects samples at mine sites **twice per month**
2. Runs **pH and specific conductance** in the field at time of collection
3. Delivers samples to **LRS in Asheville**, which performs **sulfate and TSS** analyses
4. LRS forwards samples to **Waypoint Analytical** for metals analysis — **iron, manganese, zinc, copper**
5. Receives completed analyses back via **email from LRS / Waypoint**
6. Enters results into **HMRs (Hydrologic Monitoring Reports) and DMRs**

**Note:** Results can require reanalysis when values appear atypical, which causes delays and can create potential red flags in the data.
